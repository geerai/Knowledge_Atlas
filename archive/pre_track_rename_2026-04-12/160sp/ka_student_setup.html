Your First Assignment: Collect Research Articles

Every student completes this in Week 1

Your assignment is to:

  1. Upload ten APA citations for EXPERIMENT ARTICLES — either one at a time copied into the text box, or upload a file with all 10.
  2. Upload the PDFs that correspond to those citations.
  3. We will verify that they are articles about experiments (not theory or meta-reviews) and that they are on the topic we assigned you.

Then:

  1. Create a question for Google AI Scholar on one of our CNFA Topics and repeat the same APA + PDF upload process — but this time you can upload the 10 articles you think are the best, regardless of article type.
Go to Article Upload Page → Google Scholar Search Guide
