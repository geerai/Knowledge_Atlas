Attention Restoration Theory (ART)
High VOI Kaplan & Kaplan · 1989
Cognition Nature exposure DMN engagement

Core claim: Natural environments passively engage involuntary attention and allow directed attention to replenish, producing measurable recovery on executive-function tasks after exposure.

Where to look next: Articles (14) · Mechanisms (3) · Neural underpinnings (2)

Stress Reduction Theory (SRT)
High VOI Ulrich · 1983
Physiology Autonomic

Core claim: Exposure to natural settings produces a rapid, affect-mediated decrement in autonomic arousal, independent of cognitive appraisal.

Prospect-Refuge Theory
Medium VOI Appleton · 1975
Spatial Affective

Core claim: Environments that afford both broad outward view (prospect) and sheltered vantage (refuge) are preferred because they satisfy evolved vigilance-and-safety heuristics.

Environmental Affordance Theory
Medium VOI Gibson · 1979 (ext. Heft, 2010)
Perception Action

Core claim: Perceived action possibilities of a setting are recovered directly from its geometry and material properties, without intervening cognitive inference.

Embodied Cognition in Architecture
Medium VOI Mallgrave · 2013; Robinson · 2021
Sensorimotor Metaphor

Core claim: Architectural experience is scaffolded by sensorimotor simulations of the body moving through and acting on the built environment; spatial meaning is not primarily symbolic.

Thermal Alliesthesia
Low VOI de Dear · 2011
Thermal Pleasure

Core claim: Thermal pleasure depends on the body's current thermal state, so oscillating rather than constant conditions maximise occupant satisfaction.
