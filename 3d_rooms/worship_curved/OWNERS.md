## Notes on the integration lead role

The integration lead is nominated by the instructor in Week 5 from among the strongest cohort members. The role carries:

- Authority to revise typed contracts when integration surfaces drift
- Responsibility for the cohort-wide release PR at term end
- Co-author credit on the released module

Students interested in being considered should signal in their Week 4 allocation PR description.
